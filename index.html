I am an AI research scientist focusing on AI Agents, Efficient AI, and AI for scientific discovery. Previously, I worked at Microsoft Applied Sciences, where I developed AI-based experiences for Windows Copilot, Windows Agent Arena, and WinSpot. During my tenure at MIT Lincoln Laboratory, I helped pioneer “green AI” (energy-efficient AI), focused on understanding and mitigating the energy impact of AI workloads spanning training through inference and deployment.

Selected Publications

From Words to Watts: Benchmarking the Energy Costs of Large Language Model Inference
Siddharth Samsi, Dan Zhao, Joseph McDonald, Baolin Li, Adam Michaleas, Michael Jones, William Bergeron, Jeremy Kepner, Devesh Tiwari, Vijay Gadepally
Benchmarks inference performance and energy costs for LLMs across GPU generations and multi-node settings.
Focus: measuring inference energy cost at scale (not just training) and providing practical insight for deployment tradeoffs.
Windows Agent Arena: Evaluating Multi-modal OS Agents at Scale
Rogerio Bonatti, Dan Zhao, Francesco Bonacci, Dillon Dupont, Sara Abdali, Yinheng Li, Yadong Lu, Justin Wagle, Kazuhito Koishida, Arthur Bucker, Lawrence Keunho Jang, Zheng Hui
Introduces a scalable benchmark for multimodal agents operating in a real Windows OS environment, plus analysis of agent behavior.
Includes a parallelizable evaluation setup (e.g., cloud-scale) to speed up full benchmark runs. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
Layer by Layer: Uncovering Hidden Representations in Language Models
Oscar Skean, Md Rifat Arefin, Dan Zhao, Niket Patel, Jalal Naghiyev, Yann LeCun, Ravid Shwartz-Ziv
Shows intermediate layers can encode strong representations and proposes metrics to analyze representation quality across layers.
Finds mid-layer embeddings can outperform final-layer embeddings on many embedding tasks. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
Sustainable Supercomputing for AI: GPU Power Capping at HPC Scale
Dan Zhao, Siddharth Samsi, Joseph McDonald, Baolin Li, David Bestor, Michael Jones, Devesh Tiwari, Vijay Gadepally
Studies system-wide effects of GPU power capping on temperature and power draw at supercomputing scale with minimal performance impact.
Frames power capping as a practical lever for energy-aware HPC/AI operations. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
VideoWebArena: Evaluating Long Context Multimodal Agents with Video Understanding Web Tasks
Lawrence Jang, Yinheng Li, Dan Zhao, Charles Ding, Justin Lin, Paul Pu Liang, Rogerio Bonatti, Kazuhito Koishida
Introduces a benchmark of web-agent tasks grounded in long video tutorials to test long-context video understanding for agents.
Highlights large gaps vs. human performance and challenges in skill/factual retention from video context. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
A Green(er) World for AI
Dan Zhao, Nathan C. Frey, Joseph McDonald, Matthew Hubbell, David Bestor, Michael Jones, Andrew Prout, Vijay Gadepally, Siddharth Samsi
Outlines a view of “Green AI” and practical steps across research and computing to reduce energy and environmental footprint.
