Dan Zhao

AI Research Scientist
NYU · MIT

I am an AI research scientist. I previously worked at Microsoft Applied Sciences, where I developed AI-based experiences for Windows Copilot, Windows Agent Arena, and WinSpot. During my tenure at MIT Lincoln Laboratory, I helped pioneer Green AI (energy-efficient AI), focused on understanding and mitigating the energy impact of AI workloads spanning training through inference and deployment.
