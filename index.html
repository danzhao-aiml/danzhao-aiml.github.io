I am an AI research scientist focusing on AI Agents, Efficient AI, and AI for scientific discovery. Previously, I worked at Microsoft Applied Sciences, where I developed AI-based experiences for Windows Copilot, Windows Agent Arena, and WinSpot. During my tenure at MIT Lincoln Laboratory, I helped pioneer “green AI” (energy-efficient AI), focused on understanding and mitigating the energy impact of AI workloads spanning training through inference/deployment, including work on energy costs of LLM inference.
